-   Protect your hearing! Do not use the device for extended periods at
    high volumes.

-   Do not use the device while driving, bicycling, or any other
    activity where you need to be aware of your surroundings.

-   This device is designed for indoor use only.

-   To avoid risk of fire or electric shock, do not expose the device to
    rain, moisture, or condensation.

-   Do not submerge the device in water.

-   Do not expose the device to extreme temperatures such as in a parked
    car, direct sunlight, or heating elements such as a radiator or
    stove.

-   Do not disassemble the device. There is a risk of electric shock and
    there are no user-serviceable parts inside. Diassembling the device
    will void your warranty.
