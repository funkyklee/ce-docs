- This device is designed for indoor use only.

- To avoid risk of fire or electric shock, do not expose the device to
rain, moisture, or condensation.

- To avoid overheating, do not block the ventilation holes on the back
of the device.

- Keep the device out of direct sunlight.

- Do not disassemble the device. There is a risk of electric shock and
there are no user-serviceable parts inside. Diassembling the device will
void your warranty.
