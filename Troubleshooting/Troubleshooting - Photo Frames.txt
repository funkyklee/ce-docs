- The device will not turn on.

Please ensure that the power plug is securely connected.

- The device does not detect the SD card or USB drive.

Please ensure that there is a card or USB drive installed, and that it
contains readable photo files. Empty cards and drives cannot be read.

- The device cannot play the file.

Please confirm that the file is compatible with the player. Refer to the
device specifications for more information.

- The display is too bright, too dark, the color is faded, or there is
no color.

Please adjust the display brightness, contrast, and color in the setup
menu. Avoid viewing in direct sunlight or in very bright environments.
