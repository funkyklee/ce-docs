Before using a wireless player with Bluetooth(r), you will need to
establish a connection between it and your Naxa device. This process is
called "pairing" and you will only have to do it once. Once two devices
are paired, they will automatically connect when they detect each other.

Wireless players with Bluetooth may include smartphones, tablets,
laptops, and more. Make sure that your player has its Bluetooth radio
enabled and that it is set to be discoverable.

To pair a player with your Naxa headphones:

-   Place your headphones in pairing mode: hold [MFB] until the Status
    indicator starts flashing blue and green. The NE-927 is broadcasting
    a short-range pairing signal.

-   Find the pairing signal: Use the player to search for available
    devices (refer to the player's manual to learn how to do this).
    Choose the NE-927.

-   If the player asks, use the passcode "0000".

-   The status indicator will slowly flash blue when pairing is
    complete.

Tip: Your headphones can connect to more than one device at a time. This
may be useful if you want to be ready to take calls from a cellphone and
listen to music on another device.

To connect to other devices: 1) temporarily disable Bluetooth on the
first device; 2) Pair the second device; 3) Enable Bluetooth on the
first device again and connect it to the headphones.

Antes de usar un reproductor inalámbrico con Bluetooth(r), se requiere
establecer conexión entre el reproductor y la unidad de Naxa. Este
proceso es llamado “pairing" (o "sincronía") y solo se requiere hacerlo
una vez. Una vez que los dos dispositivos están sincronizados, estos se
conectan automáticamente cuando se detectan cada uno.

Reproductores inalámbricos con función de Bluetooth pueden ser teléfonos
inteligentes, computadoras personales, tabletas electrónicas, y más.
Asegurarse que el reproductor tiene la función de Bluetooth habilitada y
que esta ajustado para el modo de búsqueda.
